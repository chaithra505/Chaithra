Pre-University Course which is also known as Plus Two is a two year course and an important stage of education with respect to the future of the Student. It is in this stage that a student begins to diversify into various specialized courses. This course is conducted by the college in accordance with the norms and regulations of Karnataka State Department of Pre-University Education. Humanities, Science and Commence are the areas of studies offered by the College.
